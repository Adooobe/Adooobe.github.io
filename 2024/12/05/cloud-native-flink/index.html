cloud-native-flink

Dec 5, 2024

Background

We have a cluster of EMR based on Hadoop and run serveral Flink Streaming job in production so far. However, to formulate the real-time data warehouse and support low-latency data needs, especially for tag services and ad-hoc queries from C-end, a robust SLA guarantee for ETL workflows is essential.

What we concerned are as follows:

  • High Available(Both clusters and jobs)
  • Failover
  • Maintenance

In conclusion, we finally decide to use cloud-native Flink to solve or improve our capabilities of streaming ETL.
